enPact Suite

One Platform.
Five Proven Technologies.

Each solution works independently or as a tailored package — installed around operations with zero downtime and verified savings from day one.

enPact Cooling & Refrigeration

Award-winning adaptive logic optimizer that intercepts thermostat signals and intelligently adjusts cooling cycles using real-time temperature data. Reduces compressor runtime without affecting performance.

Up to 40% energy reduction →

enPact Voltage Optimization

Transformer-based solution that steps down incoming voltage from 242V to an optimal ~220V — the design voltage for most equipment — delivering instant kWh savings across entire sites.

Up to 19% energy reduction →

enPact FilterPro

Whole-site voltage harmonization and waste energy filtration. Mitigates electrical noise from reactive power, load switching, and power quality issues for site-wide efficiency gains.

Full power quality optimization →

enPact Maxwell for Chillers

Advanced nanofluid heat transfer additive for chilled water systems. Improves heat exchange performance in closed-loop systems, reducing compressor load and overall system runtime.

Reduced compressor load →

Energy & Carbon Management Platform

Partnered with Univers, our real-time monitoring platform connects to sub-meters, sensors, and all enPact solutions — delivering actionable insights from high-level portfolio trends down to granular equipment performance. Track, verify, and optimize continuously.
